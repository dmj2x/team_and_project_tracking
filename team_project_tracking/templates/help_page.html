  • Registration and login

    Only invited users can register and login to this system. When you receive an invitation email and click the registration link, you will be redirected to a registration page where you will enter your information.

    On the registration form, sex, address, date of birth and bio are optional fields.

    Please note:

    The registration link in the email is a one time link and as a result, the invitee must fill out the form completely and submit it once the link is clicked.

    If the link is clicked more than once, it will redirect to a blank page. At this point, a new invitation will be requred.

  • Lost/Forgotten Password

    For lost or forgotten passwords, click on the Lost Password? link on the login page. This will prompt you to enter the email you registered with, and you will shortly receive an email with a link to reset your password.

    You must use the email you registered with, otherwise you will not receive a password reset link.

  • User profile and password management

    You can view your userprofile by clicking on your firstname in the top right corner next to the logout button.

    On clicking your firstname, you will be redirected to your profile where you can update your personal information and change your password, except your email.

    If you must update your email address, please contact Darrell, Deborah or Chana. While in your profile, you will be able to change your password, however you will be prompted to enter your current password first.

  • Inviting new users

    If you have the right privileges, you will be able to invite new users to register. You can do this by selecting Invite new user under the New Users dropdown in the menu bar.

    You can see a list of invited users under the same dropdown, New Users, by selecting the Invitations tab.

  • Communities

    You can view the list of existing communities by either clicking the MedWater logo or the Home tab on the navbar.

    In order to add a new community, click the add new community button on the home page, and submitting the form. You can get to the home page by clicking the Home tab in the menu.
    While on the home page, you can click on any community in the list of existing communities to view its details.
